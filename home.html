TRIPPLE SEVEN TIMBER TECH (PVT) LTD, A SPECIALIST JOINERY AND ROOFING COMPANY PROVIDING RELIABLE SOLUTIONS SINCE 1995

Tripple Seven Timber Tech (Pvt) Ltd, is a specialist joinery and roofing company whose main thrust is the supply of quick, reliable, and affordable roofing solutions to clients in both local and international markets. Established in 1995, the company has a proven track record in design, estimation, supply of roofing materials, and erection of all types of roofing trusses. It is manned by highly qualified staff, most of whom were trained at the University of Zimbabwe in Roof Erection to SABS 0243 & SAZ standards.
