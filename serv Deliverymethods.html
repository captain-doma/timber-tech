Design-Build Capability

Tripple Seven Timber Tech has established itself as a trusted leader in design-build construction, delivering top-tier craftsmanship for both private and public sector clients. With a strong reputation for quality and dependability, the company has become a preferred partner in custom timber structures, roofing systems, and general construction solutions. Our approach integrates design and construction into a seamless process, allowing us to deliver projects faster, more efficiently, and with greater innovation. This integrated method ensures a clear understanding of client objectives, minimizes delays, and maximizes value—qualities that have led to repeat contracts and long-term partnerships. Tripple Seven Timber Tech continues to set the standard in design-build excellence, combining skilled craftsmanship with cutting-edge techniques across Zimbabwe's construction landscape.

Project Experience

With an expanding portfolio, Tripple Seven Timber Tech has successfully completed a diverse range of commercial and residential projects. From industrial facilities to retail complexes and high-end homes, we bring quality and reliability to every job site. Notable projects include: Prevail International Factory – Complete roofing design and installation for one of the region’s prominent industrial facilities. Sam Levy’s Village Roof Refurbishment – Precision roof work on one of Harare’s most iconic retail hubs, completed with minimal disruption to ongoing operations. Highland Park Commercial Complex – Roofing and timber detailing for a modern mixed-use development. Queen of Hearts – Stylish timber structures and custom roofing for one of Harare’s top hospitality venues. These projects showcase our ability to deliver under pressure, maintain strict quality standards, and exceed client expectations. The continued trust from top-tier clients is a reflection of our professionalism, attention to detail, and relentless commitment to excellence.
